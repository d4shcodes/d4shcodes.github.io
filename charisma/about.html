Who We Are

Our story, our beliefs, and our leadership.

Our Story


LifePoint Church began with a simple vision: to create a place where people could experience the life-changing presence of God. Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant community of believers passionate about serving our city and the world.

Our Beliefs


  • The Bible: We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God.
  • God: We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • Jesus: We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God who died for our sins and rose again.
  • Salvation: We believe salvation is a free gift of God received through faith in Jesus Christ.
