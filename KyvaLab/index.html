What Changes After
60 Minutes

Fewer Risky Clicks

Teams learn to recognise phishing indicators and verify senders before acting. Links and attachments are handled cautiously across email and chat. Early reporting increases while accidental clicks decline.

MFA Adoption

Use strong passphrases with a password manager. Enable MFA on high-risk systems—email, VPN, finance, and cloud apps. Provide clear rollout steps and templates to speed uptake.

Backup Readiness

Identify critical data across laptops, servers, and cloud services. Implement the 3-2-1 backup rule and schedule brief restore tests. Define roles so restoration is fast and predictable.

AI Use Policy

Set simple do’s and don’ts for prompts and data sharing with AI tools. Clarify what is allowed, what is sensitive, and when to anonymise data. Offer a one-page checklist to reinforce daily practice.
